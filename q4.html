Pros and Cons of PNG:

  • Pros of PNG:
    • Lossless Compression
    • Transparency Support
    • Great for Graphics
    • Wide Browser Support
    • Higher Quality
  • Cons of PNG:
    • File Size
    • Limited Photography Use
    • Not Ideal for Printing
    • Limited Color Space
    • Compatibility with Older Software

Pros and Cons of JPG:

  • Pros of JPG:
    • High Compression
    • Photography-friendly
    • Printing Adaptability
    • Widely Supported
    • Adjustable Compression
  • Cons of JPG:
    • Lossy Compression
    • No Transparency
    • Not Ideal for Graphics
    • Limited Editing
    • Color Loss
